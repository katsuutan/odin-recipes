Tamago-kake Gohan

Descriptions

Tamago-kake gohan is a combination of Japanese rice with a raw egg. It's made by simply cracking an egg over a steaming bowl of rice and then mixing it with chopsticks. The egg slightly thickens as it is beaten, due to the heat from the rice.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Egg
  • (Optional) Green Onions
  • (Optional) Furikake

Steps

  1. Wash rice grain thoroughly.
  2. Add the rice to rice cooker using 1:1 method.
  3. Once rice is cooked, quickly add rice to a serving bowl.
  4. Create a small indent in the middle of the rice, and crack an egg on top of it.
  5. Start mixing the egg into the rice throughly.
  6. (Optional) Add green onion and furikake to your liking.
