Sashimi

Description

A recipe for a nicely plated platter of salmon sashimi typically seen in Japanese restaurants.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Sashimi Grade Salmon
  • Soy Sauce
  • Wasabi

Steps

  1. Slice the salmon with about half an inch of thickness each. Width may vary based on preference.
  2. Add soy sauce to a small sauce plate
  3. Plate the salmon on a nice dish.
  4. (Optional) Add wasabi to the side of the plate for aesthetic.
  5. (Optional) Mix wasabi with soy sauce for a superior taste.
